2 Cool 4 Skool

2 Cool 4 Skool is the debut single album by BTS. It was released on June 12, 2013 with "No More Dream" serving as the single's title track. "We Are Bulletproof Pt.2" was used as a follow-up track during promotions.


O!RUL8,2?

O!RUL8,2? is the first mini-album by BTS. It was released on September 11, 2013 with "N.O" serving as the album's title track.

The album was the fifty-fifth best-selling album of 2013 in South Korea.


Skool Luv Affair

Skool Luv Affair is the second mini album by BTS. The album was released on February 12, 2014 with "Boy In Luv" serving as the album's title track. "Just One Day" was used as a follow-up track during promotions.

The album was the twentieth best-selling album of 2014 in South Korea. As of 2020, the album has sold more than three hundred seventy-four thousand copies alone and with the repackaged version more than one million copies.


Skool Luv Affair: Special Addition

Skool Luv Affair Special Addition is a repackage of BTS' second mini album Skool Luv Affair. It was released on May 14, 2014 with "Miss Right" serving as the album's title track and contains an additional two new songs and six hidden tracks.

The album originally was released with a limited amount of copies, but was re-printed and re-released on October 13, 2020.


Dark & Wild

Dark & Wild is the first studio album by BTS. It was released on August 19, 2014, with "Danger" serving as the album's title track. "War of Hormone" was used as a follow-up track during promotions.

The album was the fourteenth best-selling album of 2014 in South Korea.


The Most Beautiful Moment In Life Pt.1

The Most Beautiful Moment in Life Pt.1 is the third mini album by BTS. It was released on April 29, 2015, with "I Need U" serving as the album's title track. "Dope" was used as a follow-up track during promotions.

The album was the sixth best-selling album of 2015 in South Korea.


The Most Beautiful Moment In Life Pt.2

The Most Beautiful Moment in Life Pt.2 is the fourth mini album by BTS. It was released on November 30, 2015 with "Run" serving as the album's title track.

The album was the fifth best-selling album of 2015 in South Korea.


The Most Beautiful Moment In Life: Young Forever

The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Young Forever is the first compilation album by BTS. It was released on May 2, 2016 with "Fire" serving as the album's title track. "Save Me" was used as a follow-up track during promotions. "Epilogue: Young Forever" serves also as the third single from their album. The album contains the tracks from their two previous mini albums, three new songs and several new remixes.

The album was the fourth best-selling album of 2016 in South Korea.


WINGS

Wings is the second studio album by BTS. It was released on October 10, 2016 with "Blood Sweat & Tears" serving as the album's title track.

The album was the best-selling album of 2016 in South Korea. As of 2020, the album has sold more than one million copies alone and with the repackaged version more than two million copies.


You Never Walk Alone

You Never Walk Alone is a repackage of BTS' second studio album Wings. It was released on February 13, 2017 with "Spring Day" serving as the album's title track.

The album was the third best-selling album of 2017 in South Korea. As 2020, the album has sold more than one million copies alone and with the original edition more than two million copies.


Love Yourself: Her

Love Yourself: Her is the fifth mini album by BTS. It was released on September 18, 2017 with "DNA" serving as the album's title track.

The album debuted at #7 on Billboard 200.

The album was the best-selling album of 2017 in South Korea. It is currently the tenth best-selling album on South Korea of all time.

As 2020, the album has sold more than two million copies.


Love Yourself: Tear

Love Yourself: Tear is the third studio album by BTS. It was released on May 18, 2018 with "FAKE LOVE" serving as the album's title track.

The album debuted at #1 on Billboard 200, becoming BTS' first #1 on that chart.

The album was a nominee for the GRAMMYs "Best Recording Package".

The album was the second best-selling album of 2018 in South Korea. It is currently the nineth best-selling album on South Korea of all time.

As 2020, the album has sold more than two million copies.


Love Yourself: Answer

Love Yourself: Answer is the second compilation album by BTS. It was released on August 24, 2018 with "IDOL" serving as the album's title track. The album contains the tracks from their two previous albums, seven new songs and three new remixes.

The album debuted at #1 on Billboard 200, becoming BTS's second #1 on that chart.

The album was the best-selling album of 2018 in South Korea and second best-selling album of 2018 in the world. This album is currently the fourth best-selling album on South Korea of all time.

As 2021, the album has sold more than two and half million copies.


Map of the Soul: Persona

Map of the Soul: Persona is the sixth mini album by BTS. It was released on April 12, 2019 with "Boy With Luv" serving as the album's title track.

The album debuted at #1 on Billboard 200, becoming BTS's third #1 on that chart.

This album was the best-selling album of 2019 in South Korean history and third best-selling album of 2019 in the world. This album is currently the second best-selling album in South Korea of all time, with Map of the Soul: 7 being number one.

As of 2021, the album has sold more than three million copies, almost four million.


BTS World Original Soundtrack

BTS World: Original Soundtrack is the soundtrack album by BTS for their Netmarble mobile game BTS World. It was released on June 28, 2019 with "Heartbeat" serving as the album's title track.

This album was the fifth best-selling album of 2019 in South Korea.


Map of the Soul: 7

Map of the Soul: 7 is the fourth studio album by BTS. It was released on February 21, 2020, with "ON" serving as the album's title track.

The album debuted at #1 on Billboard 200, becoming BTS's fourth #1 on that chart.

This album was the best-selling album of 2020 in South Korea. This album is currently the best-selling album in South Korea of all time.

As of 2021, the album has sold more than four million copies.


BE

BE is the first self-directed and seventh mini-album by BTS. It was released on November 20, 2020, at 2PM KST.

The album debuted at #1 on Billboard 200, becoming BTS's fifth #1 on that chart.

This album was the second best-selling album of 2020 in South Korea. This album is currently the fifth best best-selling album in South Korea of all time.

As of 2021, the album has sold more than two and half million copies.


Butter

Butter (known digitally as Butter/Permission to Dance) is the first English single album by BTS. It was released on July 9, 2021.

It was certified Platinum for selling more than 250,000 copies by the RIAJ in July 2021.
